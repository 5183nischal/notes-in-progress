title: Moon Strike
---

To[[night]], the waning moon  
rose into a [[light]]ning storm,  
rolling thunder over a [[white]] [[water]] road:  
these few words my [[hope]]less offering  
in service of a bright [[sea]]  
struck by night's k[[now]]ing.
