title: Sky Letters
---

The sky will read you  
the [[secret]] start to every _surah_  
if you stop and rest [[here]], seeker.  
  
No need for anything  
but to see it, swirling in the high  
jetstream scrolled across  
the [[heaven]]s, dyed now  
in divinity.
